## What Alta does

Alta is an AI-powered outbound calling platform that helps B2B SaaS sales teams book
more qualified meetings without adding SDR headcount.

## Core value propositions

1. **Instant scale** — Alta can make hundreds of personalised outbound calls per day,
   running 24/7 without ramp time or sick days.

2. **Consistent messaging** — Every call follows your exact pitch, handles objections
   to your playbook, and books directly onto your sales team's calendar.

3. **Human-quality conversations** — Powered by advanced speech-to-speech AI (not a
   robocall), Alta's agents sound natural, respond in real time, and adapt to the
   prospect's tone.

4. **Full compliance built in** — Every call opens with a clear AI disclosure, honours
   do-not-call lists, and respects calling-hours windows.

5. **Easy integration** — Connects to your CRM and calendar in under a day; no complex
   setup or IT involvement required.
